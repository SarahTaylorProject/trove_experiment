With the increase in staff in the building it has come to our attention about the cleanliness of the kitchen.
We understand that people are often busy at work 
and do not stop to take the time to clean up after they've eaten lunch or a snack 
This is why it is very important that all staff pitch in 
and work together to ensure that everyone has a clean place to eat and take a break
If you leave food in the refrigerator and do not eat it within the work week 
either throw it out or take it home
This not only makes sure the refrigerator does not get overloaded, 
but it helps prevent the food from rotting and smelling up the refrigerator 
If you use plates, forks, spoons, or cups from the kitchen
rinse all dishes immediately after use and before putting them in the dishwasher
If you spill something on the floor, clean it up immediately 
and make sure the floor is dry to avoid slipping hazards


Clean leaks and spills immediately
Wipe down the sandwich press after use
Rinse dishes before putting them in the dishwasher
Put your rubbish in the bin
Remember to dispose of expired or spoiled food 

Leave solid dishes in the sink
leave food scraps in the sink
Leave the sandwich press on
Leave a mess for someone else to clean up
