Our thoughts are with our colleagues and their families that have been and are still affected by the bushfires across our nation. If you’d like to help our community or connect with others at this time, please join ‘Bushfire crisis – how we can help’. 
This year marks the final year of Program Transformation as a strategic project. It’s important to recognise however that continuous improvement of our programs will continue beyond the life of the project.
At the end of 2020, a Program Transformation Framework will be rolled out across the University to support all our program teams to embed ongoing program transformation in our day to day operations. This will include built-in capacity in workload planning for staff as well as built-in processes for evaluating and reporting on the impact of our ongoing program transformation work.
Our focus this year will be:
•	consolidating the work we have been doing to embed RMIT’s Program Principles in our 74 higher education programs in scope, with a particular focus on the remaining three principles
•	designing and piloting a sustainable Program Transformation Framework for continuous program transformation beyond 2020
•	transitioning program transformation into the daily operations of colleges, schools and program teams.
As part of the learning and teaching community, many of you and your colleagues will be contributing to this project through
•	participation in college delivery teams to support the program uplift work
•	lending your expertise and knowledge to input into the design of the Program Transformation Framework
•	supporting program teams within your college to pilot the future framework. 
Planning is underway to determine resourcing and workflow for the year. More information about how we will all work together this year will be provided to you at college information sessions in the coming weeks. 
In the meantime if you have any questions, please reach out to your college or Studios Project Manager (Dhriti Banerji for SEH, Matt Sherman for CoBL, Peter Muir for DSC, Sally Parrott for Studios) or the Program Transformation team at program.transformation@rmit.edu.au
